About Us

P. S. Diesel Spares

Commenced in the year 1994, we, “P. S. Diesel Spares”, are an illustrious organization, engaged as a manufacturer, exporter, supplier, wholesaler, trader, retailer and importer of a commendable range of Metal Precision Products. The product-line offered by us comprises Cummins Engine Components, Cummins Product and Controller Product. The products offered by us are widely acknowledged in the market for their features such as robust construction, corrosion resistance, high strength, compact design and lightweight. Apart from this, we are rendering Testing Service to our customers, which is inclusive of Industrial Generator Testing Service and Industrial Machinery Testing Service.

To meet the expectations of customers, we have hired a team of dexterous professionals, who hold extensive knowledge of this domain. Our professionals manufactured the entire range in adherence with the international quality standards. These professionals are responsible to run our business operations smoothly. In addition, we provide our team with regular training sessions to keep them updated with the changing market dynamics. Also, our team renders the services as per the guidelines laid in the industry to attain highest benchmarks of quality. We are exporting our products in all over the world and also are importing our products from Singapore, U.S.A, Taiwan, Japan & China.

Under the guidance of our mentor 'Mr. Pradeepkumar Shaw, we have mustered formidable position across the national and international markets. Owing to the hard work and dedication of our mentor, we gained an edge over our competitors.
